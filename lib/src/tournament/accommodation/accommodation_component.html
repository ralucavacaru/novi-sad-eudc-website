Accommodation

All participants will be accommodated either at Hotel Park or at Hotel Leopold I. Most participants will be accommodated in double and triple rooms, there will also be a few mini appartments consisting of several rooms. You will be able to indicate your rooming preference during individual registration, which will happen at the beginning of the summer, and we will do our best to accommodate your wishes.

The venues will be between the hotels, at similar distances. We will provide transfers from and to both hotels.

Both hotels have fitness and wellness centers, as well as restaurants and bars. Hotel Park also has a pool.
